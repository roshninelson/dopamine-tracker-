Dopamine Level Chart

What Dopamine Levels Indicate

1-3 (Low): Activities that might feel draining, unfulfilling, or even lead to procrastination. These could be things you *have* to do but don't enjoy, or passive activities that don't provide much mental stimulation or sense of accomplishment.

4-6 (Moderate): Activities that are neutral or mildly enjoyable. They might be routine tasks, light entertainment, or things that provide a small sense of satisfaction.

7-8 (Good): Activities that genuinely make you feel engaged, happy, and productive. These often involve a sense of achievement, learning, or social connection.

9-10 (High): Activities that bring you immense joy, deep focus, or a strong sense of purpose and accomplishment. These are often highly rewarding and leave you feeling energized and fulfilled.

Remember, these levels are subjective and serve as a tool for personal reflection.
